About FREDKA CONSULT

FREDKA Consult is a private consultancy firm based in Accra, established in 2014 under the Companies Code. We stand for quality healthcare management and HR services that deliver cost-effective solutions.

Our mission is to ensure organizations have employees with the requisite knowledge and skills that foster productivity and growth. We provide total HR services through collaboration and high-quality delivery that exceeds expectations.

Our Vision

To be a healthcare management and human resource consultancy firm with an efficient and competent team ready to deliver quality training and staffing services that meet required standards.

Our Mission

To collaborate and improve the knowledge and skill status of staff by recruiting, selecting, and training competent employees within any industry through high-quality HR services.
